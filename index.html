Hi. I'm Andreu Botella (pronouns he/they), a full-stack web developer focusing on the latest and greatest of the web platform, as well as a contributor to the WHATWG and W3C web platform standards, to the Firefox browser, and to the Deno Javascript runtime.

My work on development includes experience working with languages, runtimes and frameworks such as Node.js, React, Next.js, Express.js, PHP. My work on the web platform includes formalizing the internals of how character encodings are processed, getting browsers to agree on how to normalize line breaks in form submissions, and working on a precise definition of the multipart/form-data enctype that can be used in web standards.

Other interests of mine include math, physics, linguistics, music, and LGBTQ+ activism.
